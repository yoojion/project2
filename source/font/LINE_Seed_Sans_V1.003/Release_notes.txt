Basic information
1 Font name: LINE Seed Sans
2 TrueType Version record: 1.003
3 Regional package: Latin
4 Supported scripts: Latin
5 Number of glyphs: 176
6 Weights/Styles: Thin, Regular, Bold, ExtraBold, Heavy
7 Usage: Text and Display

Technology
1 File formats: The files have been delivered in the TrueType-based and in PostScript-based OpenType format, for use in all major Operating Systems: TTF; OTF. Also as TTF for use in apps, and in web font format: WOFF; WOFF2; EOT.
2 Embedding settings: Editing of the document is allowed (editable mode), subsetting is allowed.
3 Hinting information: The glyphs are manually hinted for optimized rasterization in Microsoft ClearType or for other LCD devices. The icons (camera, chat, envelope, LINEtimeline, music, phone, search, shopping, smile, speaker, user, LINEApp) and logos (LINEWordmark, LINECopyright) are not hinted.
4 Default numerals: Proportional.
5 PUAs are assigned to the following glyphs: LINEApp E001, LINEWordmark E002, LINECopyright E003
